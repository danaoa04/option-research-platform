# Portfolio Guide

The portfolio workspace focuses on offline portfolio snapshots and exposure
review.

## What you can inspect

- positions and strategy cycles;
- delta, gamma, theta, and vega exposure;
- concentration by group;
- event risk;
- assignment risk;
- diagnostics;
- reproducibility checksums.

## How to use it

1. Open a fixture portfolio snapshot.
2. Filter positions and inspect a single position detail panel.
3. Review exposures by sector, symbol, expiration, or strategy family.
4. Carry the snapshot into Risk Lab or Replay for deeper analysis.

## Limits

- Fixture positions are not brokerage accounts.
- Exposure values are deterministic research outputs.
- No workflow in this guide can route or stage an order.
