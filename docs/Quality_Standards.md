# Quality Standards

## Overview

The platform will follow rigorous quality standards to ensure correctness, reproducibility, maintainability, and professional usability.

## Standards

- Use Ruff, Black, MyPy, pytest, and pre-commit hooks for consistent code quality.
- Maintain comprehensive documentation for product scope, architecture, workflows, and operations.
- Require reproducible backtests with data snapshot, software version, configuration, parameters, and random seed.
- Validate Greeks, pricing, assignment, margin, execution, and benchmark performance.
- Keep user-facing research outputs transparent, auditable, and exportable.

## Review Expectations

- Every significant feature should include documentation, tests, and validation notes.
- Backtesting and research workflows should be repeatable and traceable.
- Security, privacy, and operational resilience should be considered for every major subsystem.
