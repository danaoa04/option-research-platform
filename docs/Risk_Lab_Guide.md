# Risk Lab Guide

Risk Lab provides deterministic stress analysis over fixture portfolios.

## Main tools

- scenario catalogue;
- scenario builder;
- spot and IV shocks;
- liquidity and margin shocks;
- scenario matrix;
- limit breaches;
- remediation comparisons.

## Workflow

1. Choose a portfolio.
2. Select a scenario preset or configure custom shocks.
3. Validate the scenario.
4. Acknowledge that the run is synthetic and not a forecast.
5. Review matrix cells, limit breaches, and remediation candidates.

## Interpreting output

- Scenario results are stress analyses, not probabilities.
- Limit breaches help compare risk states, not predict exact losses.
- Remediation candidates are research comparisons only.
