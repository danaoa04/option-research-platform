# Glossary

## Overview

This glossary defines common terms used across the platform documentation.

## Terms

- Backtest: A replay of a strategy over historical market data.
- Greeks: Sensitivities of option value to model inputs such as price, volatility, time, and interest.
- IV surface: A representation of implied volatility across strike and expiration dimensions.
- Multi-leg strategy: A strategy composed of multiple options and/or stock legs.
- Reproducibility: The ability to reproduce a research result from a known dataset, configuration, parameters, and seed.
- Scenario simulator: A tool for analyzing portfolio behavior under hypothetical market conditions.
